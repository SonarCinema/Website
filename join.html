Freshers Fayre 2019

Thank you for showing your interest at Sonar CInema. If you scanned the QR code on our stall you should've been redirected to this page. Here you'll find more details on what we do and what roles you will forfill. We are hosting an Introduction event on the 27th of September in LT1 at around 6pm where will explain more of what we are doing this year and what to expect from the society, you don't need to be a member to attend this event so if you have friends who you feel may be interested, please do invite them to come along. 

Our crew training sessions will be on the following week, where we will be going through and training you all with the projector as well as what to do on a typical showing. Days and times for these sessions are TBC.

Below are some more details should you require:

We are Solent University’s Cinema society, we run LT1 (Lecture Theatre 1) as a cinema / events location. We have two main roles that you forfill as a member of the crew:

 - Front of House: Where you greet customers & help them with any questions they may have.

 - Projection: Where you run the projector and manage the technical side of things on the showing

We provide full training for both roles at training sessions at the start of each semester. If you have missed these sessions please email us (sonarcinema@solentsu.co.uk) and we'll arrange a date and time to show you the ropes. We are running a selection of events this year ranging from showing films publically, gaming sessions, private showings, society socials & more. You also get relevant experience which can help you in getting a job at bigger cinema's as well as looking good on your CV. 

If you are interested in joining us but want to give it a go first, please get in touch with us via our email (sonarcinema@solentsu.co.uk) where we will be happy to arrange a taster session for you to get a good idea of what we do before joining officially. 

If you would like to join the society, the joining fee is £5 which is to be paid in the SU, follow the link below,
